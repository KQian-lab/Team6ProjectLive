Welcome To Your Flight Deck

Use the arrow keys to control your ship and the spacebar to blast your enemies out of the sky.

Your Objective Is Survival

"?**NAME**?" is an action-packed galactic shooter that tests your reflexes and skills as you pilot your spacecraft through a relentless onslaught of alien invaders. Taking control of your ship's weapons systems, you'll blast across the cosmos, facing off against wave after wave of hostile forces.

Meteors will rain down upon you like enemy fire.

The Cosmic Battlefield Is Harsh And Unforgiving

Your skills will be pushed to the limit as you fly through the onslaught of incoming alien fire and dodge meteors - be aware of noxious gas fields that erupt to impair your vision and make it difficult to track the very hazards that mercilessly shred your lives.

Flying through a gas field will make it impossible to see your enemy.

You Are Not Alone

Be on the lookout for power-ups that could mean the difference between saving the galaxy and burning debris. Stealth mods will temporarily cloak your ship, making you invisible to the enemy and causing them to hold their fire.
Repair packs will arrive like a stellar lifeline to restore your ship's defenses and give you a second chance to turn the tide of the battle.

Keep an eye out for repairs.
